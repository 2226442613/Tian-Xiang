About Tianxiang International

Tianxiang International is a leading import and export company specializing in construction materials, dedicated to providing high-quality products and tailored solutions to clients worldwide.

With years of industry experience, a strong global network, and a professional team, we ensure that our materials meet the highest standards and are delivered reliably and efficiently. We focus on flooring, lighting, building supplies, and more, always putting our clients first, driving innovation, and striving for excellence in everything we do.
